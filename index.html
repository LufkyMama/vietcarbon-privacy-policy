VietCarbon – Privacy Policy

Your daily CO₂ tracker and sustainability companion.
Last updated: 18 November 2025

VietCarbon (“we”, “our”, “us”, or “the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the VietCarbon mobile application.

By downloading, installing, or using the VietCarbon app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use certain features, you may provide us with:

  • Name or nickname
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (e.g. when using Google Sign-In)
  • Password or login credentials (stored in encrypted form only)

1.2 Activity & CO₂ Tracking Data

To calculate and visualize your daily CO₂ emissions, VietCarbon collects the information you manually enter into the App, for example:

  • Transportation activities (type of vehicle, approximate distance or duration)
  • Food consumption (type of food, portion size or quantity)
  • Plastic usage (type and amount of plastic items)

This information is used solely for estimating your CO₂ emissions, generating charts, and providing personalized tips and challenges.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • App usage data (screens visited, features used, time spent in the App)
  • Error reports, crash logs, and performance data

This data is collected mainly through third-party services such as Firebase, Expo services, and Google Play Services to help us improve the App.

1.4 Login and Authentication Data

If you choose to sign in using Google or another identity provider, we may receive:

  • Your name or display name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile image URL

We do not access your full account, emails, or any other unrelated personal data from your Google account.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your VietCarbon account
  • To calculate and display your daily CO₂ emissions and historical trends
  • To provide personalized challenges and suggestions to reduce emissions
  • To show leaderboards and rankings (using only non-sensitive profile information)
  • To maintain and improve the App’s performance, stability, and user experience
  • To monitor for abuse, protect against fraud, and ensure the security of the App
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforcement requests where applicable

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (Where Applicable)

Depending on your jurisdiction, our legal bases for processing your information may include:

  • Your consent (for example, when you create an account or enable certain features)
  • Performance of a contract (providing the App and its core features to you)
  • Legitimate interests (improving the App, preventing abuse, analytics)
  • Compliance with legal obligations

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your data is stored using secure cloud services such as Firebase and Google Cloud. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption in transit using HTTPS
  • Storing passwords only in hashed / encrypted form
  • Limiting access to databases to authorized personnel and services
  • Regular monitoring and maintenance of our systems

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Sharing of Your Information

5.1 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers that help us operate and improve the App, including:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Database, Analytics, Crashlytics)
  • Expo and related services
  • Google Play Services

These service providers are only allowed to use your data as needed to provide services to us and are not permitted to use it for their own independent purposes.

5.2 Leaderboards and Social Features

For features such as leaderboards or rankings, we may display limited profile information such as your chosen display name and your CO₂ performance indicators. We do not publicly share your email address or sensitive personal information.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g. a court or government agency), or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have some or all of the following rights:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • The right to request deletion of your account and data
  • The right to withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent
  • The right to restrict or object to certain types of processing
  • The right to request a copy of your data in a portable format, where feasible

You can exercise many of these rights directly within the App (for example, by deleting activities or your account). You may also contact us using the details provided in the Contact Us section below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App and its features.

If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, subject to any legal obligations requiring us to retain certain information.

8. Children's Privacy

VietCarbon is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action to delete the information.

9. International Data Transfers

Our servers and service providers may be located in different countries. By using the App, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, which may have different data protection laws. We take steps to ensure that your data is handled securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. We may also provide additional notice within the App if the changes are material.

Your continued use of the App after the updated Policy has been posted means that you accept the changes.
